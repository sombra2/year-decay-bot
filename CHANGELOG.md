## v1.0.0

Initial public release.

- Daily automated posting (once per day, enforced)
- Year progress calculated with two-decimal precision
- Visual progress bar reflecting elapsed year
- Daily weather summary for Madrid
- Apparent temperature and daylight duration reporting
- Gradually shifting tone as the year advances
- Externalized phrase system for long-term variety
- Anti-repetition logic to avoid obvious reuse
- Occasional silence days by design
- Special end-of-year message on December 31st

The bot is intentionally non-interactive and will not respond to user input.
